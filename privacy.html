2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, and password. You may also choose to provide a profile photo or additional profile information.

Health and Fitness Information: To provide our core service, we collect information about your exercise routines, fitness goals, progress tracking data, and any health-related information you choose to input. This may include information about injuries, recovery status, and wellness preferences across Mind, Body, and Soul categories.

User-Generated Content: When you use our social features, we collect the routines you create and share, text posts, and interactions with other users' content.

Communications: If you contact us for support or feedback, we collect the content of your messages along with your contact information.

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

Usage Data: We collect information about how you interact with the Service, including features used, routines accessed, time spent in the App, and actions taken.

Device Information: We collect device type, operating system, unique device identifiers, and mobile network information.

Log Data: Our servers automatically record information when you use the Service, including your IP address, access times, and app crashes or errors.

2.3 Information from Third Parties

If you choose to link a third-party account (such as Apple or Google for sign-in), we may receive basic profile information from that service according to your privacy settings with that provider.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

Provide and maintain the Service: This includes creating and managing your account, delivering personalized exercise routines, tracking your progress, and enabling social features.

Improve and develop the Service: We analyze usage patterns to understand how users interact with the App, identify issues, and develop new features.
